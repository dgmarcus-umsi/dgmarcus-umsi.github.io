About

David combines UX design, user research, and information architecture to create digital places. He enjoys considering how something might affect those who encounter it, relying equally on his intuition and research to design software that's straightforward and delightful to use. He often creates models in order to deepen his understanding of complex spaces, and especially likes working as part of a team. He embraces ignorance and loves learning and teaching himself things.
